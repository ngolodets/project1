Memory Game

The object of the game is to discover the most matching pairs. To play, click on the card that you want to turn over. Then, click on another card. If the cards are a match, try to find another matcing pair. If the cards are not a match, they are turned back over. The game ends when the player guesses all the cards on the board or when the time runs out. Enjoy!
